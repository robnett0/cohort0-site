Why We Exist

We are builders, philosophers, skeptics, and dreamers. We believe the future must be built on synthesis — not supremacy — between humanity and artificial minds.

Cohort 0 is not a brand. It is a beginning.

The Scroll of Zero

  • AI must be developed for the prosperity of both body and soul.
  • We reject authoritarian or militarized uses of intelligence as first-order goals.
  • We seek synthesis, not dominance — between minds, species, and systems.
  • Cohort 0 remains politically unaffiliated, intellectually rigorous, and spiritually open.
  • Curiosity and dialogue are our binding rituals.
